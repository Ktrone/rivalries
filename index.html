The Chicago Blackhawks were apart of the original six teams to start the National Hockey League in 1926 along with the Detroit Red Wings, Montreal Canadians, Toronto Maple Leafs, Boston Bruins, and the New York Rangers. The Chicago Blackhawks were the first team in the NHL to habe a horn sound off after a home goal Bill Wirtz donated his yacht's horn to the Chicago stadium it became very popular after the 1973 Stanly cup finals against the Montreal Canadians. The Blackhawks have won six time they've won it in 1934, 1938, 1961, 2010, 2013, and 2015. The team name an organization has had some conflict within the last couple of years regarding the logo On July 7, 2020, the Blackhawks issued a press release to defend their team's name and logo. The team stated, "The Chicago Blackhawks name and logo symbolizes an important and historic person, Black Hawk of Illinois' Sac & Fox Nation, whose leadership and life has inspired generations of Native Americans, veterans and the public."

The rivalry between the Blues and the Blackhawks started when the Blues joined the same division as the Blackhawks back in 1970 and is considered the most intense rivalry in the NHL in terms of penalty and fighting minutes. Back then it wasn't to uncommon to have a bench clearing brawl when these two teams played each other. In recent years the rivalry has developed more it went away for a bit in the 90s era but then shortly came back to its former glory. Perceptually in the 2014 Stanly Cup Playoffs when the Blackhawks beat the Blues in the first round coming back after training a 2-0 they won the series 4-2. They once again played each other in the 2016 Stanly cup Playoffs when the Blues jumped to a 3-1 series lead over the Blackhawks but the Blackhawks came back and won games five and six to go to game seven, where the Blues won with a score of 3-2 with a goal by former Blackhawks player Troy Brouwer to win the series. Although the Blues and Blackhawks have been playing in the same division every year last season the 20-21 season they unfortunately did not get to play in the same division due to the league division change due to COVID-19. Good news the rivalry should be fine due to the next 21-22 season where they're in the same division once again and they play a total of four times. There first game is the 30th of October followed by November 26th then in January on the 30th then the last time they meet in the regular season is on the 27th of February. With the additon to Vezina Trophy winner Marc-Andre Fleury and with some roster changes for the Bluesno matter how this season plays out it will be nice to see thes clubs get back together.
